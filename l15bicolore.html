L15 BICOLORE

THE L15 BICOLORE is a double bed designed by Luigi Caccia Dominioni. The Bed have black lacquer headboard and legs, and shaped insert in glossy yellow maple-wood, walnut or cherry-wood.

L15 Bicolore

First of all i realized the Headboard. The HeadBoard id made of two part: The black down part (right image) and The bicolor up part (left image). The up part is in turn composed by two other parts. The brown part is embedded on the black up part.
